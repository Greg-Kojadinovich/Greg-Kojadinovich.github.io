Color Wheel — by Europa Waters LLC

Your Eye. Your Palette. Mastered.

Most digital color tools are built for screens, not studios. Color Wheel is a plein air painting learning application engineered around subtractive RYB pigment physics — the color system that governs every stroke on a real canvas. Hold it up to the landscape, identify your hues, and know exactly what to mix before your brush ever touches paint. Built by Europa Waters LLC for artists who take the craft seriously.
