ColorWheel

SwiftUI  ·  iOS 17+  ·  RYB Color Theory

A free iOS app for painters learning color theory — built with SwiftUI and powered by real subtractive RYB pigment mixing. Whether you're squeezing paint for the first time or deepening your understanding of how colors behave on canvas, ColorWheel is a quiet companion in your studio.

🎡
Mixer

Drag any two colors on the interactive wheel and watch them blend using the same RYB model painters have used for centuries — Oil, Acrylic, or Watercolor mediums.

🖌️
Palette

23 named artist pigments — Cadmium Red, Ultramarine, Sap Green, Burnt Umber, and more — organized by family with a Mixing Bench and harmony notes.

📖
Theory

A reference guide covering primaries, secondaries, complementary pairs, analogous harmony, warm vs. cool, and how each medium behaves differently.
